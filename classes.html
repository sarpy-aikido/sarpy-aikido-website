  • De-escalation and situational awareness

What to Expect at Our Dojo

What to Wear

Comfortable athletic clothing for your first class. As you progress, you'll have the option to purchase a traditional gi (uniform).

Training Environment

Clean mats, safety equipment, and a culture of mutual respect. We push each other to improve while maintaining a safe training space.

Training Philosophy

Progressive resistance—start slow, increase intensity as you develop skill. Every technique is tested to ensure it works, not just looks good.
